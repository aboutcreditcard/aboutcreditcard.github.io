How to Pay Your Best Buy Credit Card by Mail: A Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Home
  2. Blog
  3. Blog Details

Paying your Best Buy Credit Card by mail might seem like an old-school method in today’s digital age, but it remains a reliable option—especially for those who prefer paper trails or lack consistent internet access. With rising concerns about cybersecurity, inflation, and financial literacy, understanding all payment methods is more important than ever.

This guide will walk you through the entire process, from preparing your payment to ensuring it arrives safely. Plus, we’ll touch on why mailing payments still matters in 2024.

Why Paying by Mail Still Matters in the Digital Era

Cybersecurity Concerns

With data breaches and online fraud on the rise, many consumers are wary of entering payment details online. Mailing a check or money order eliminates the risk of digital theft—though it does come with its own precautions (more on that later).

Financial Inclusion

Not everyone has reliable internet access or a smartphone. For older adults or those in rural areas, mailing payments ensures they can manage their finances without barriers.

Record-Keeping Benefits

A physical check or money order provides a paper trail, which can be helpful for budgeting, tax purposes, or disputes.

Step 1: Gather Your Payment Materials

Before sending your payment, make sure you have everything you need:

What You’ll Need:

  • Your Best Buy Credit Card statement (to confirm the payment address and account number)
  • A check or money order (personal checks are fine, but money orders are safer if you’re concerned about fraud)
  • A pen (to write your account number on the check or money order)
  • An envelope (standard letter-sized is fine)
  • A stamp (current USPS postage rates apply)

Step 2: Write Your Check or Money Order Correctly

For Checks:

  • Make it payable to “Best Buy Credit Card”
  • Write your 16-digit Best Buy Credit Card account number in the memo line
  • Sign the check

For Money Orders:

  • Purchase from a trusted source (USPS, Walmart, or your bank)
  • Fill it out similarly to a check, with your account number noted

Pro Tip: Double-check the payment amount and account number—errors can delay processing.

Step 3: Address the Envelope Properly

The mailing address for Best Buy Credit Card payments depends on whether you’re using regular mail or expedited services:

Standard Mail:

Best Buy Credit Card P.O. Box 9001007 Louisville, KY 40290-1007

Overnight/Express Mail:

Best Buy Credit Card Attn: Consumer Payment Dept. 6716 Grade Lane Building 9, Suite 910 Louisville, KY 40213

Note: Always verify the address on your latest statement, as it may change.

Step 4: Mail Your Payment with Confidence

Timing Is Key

  • Standard Mail: Send at least 7-10 business days before your due date to avoid late fees.
  • Overnight Mail: Useful if you’re cutting it close, but more expensive.

Tracking Your Payment

Consider using USPS Certified Mail or a tracking service for peace of mind. This way, you’ll have proof of delivery if any issues arise.

Step 5: Confirm Your Payment Was Processed

After mailing your payment, keep an eye on your account:

  • Check your next statement for the payment posting.
  • Log in to your Best Buy Credit Card account online (if you have access) to monitor the status.
  • Call customer service if the payment doesn’t appear within 10 business days.

Common Pitfalls to Avoid

Late Payments

  • Postal delays happen—don’t wait until the last minute.
  • If you’re close to the due date, consider an alternative method (online or phone payment).

Lost or Stolen Checks

  • Use a secure mailbox (USPS blue boxes or post office drop-offs are safest).
  • Avoid sending cash—it’s untraceable.

Incorrect Information

  • A missing account number or wrong address can delay processing. Always verify details.

Alternatives to Mailing Payments

If you’re worried about timing or security, consider these options:

Online Payments

  • Fast, free, and eco-friendly.
  • Set up autopay to avoid missed due dates.

Phone Payments

  • Call 1-888-574-1301 and follow the prompts.
  • May incur a fee if using a live agent.

In-Store Payments

  • Visit a Best Buy store and pay at the checkout counter.

Final Thoughts on Financial Responsibility

Managing credit card payments—whether by mail or digitally—is a crucial part of financial health. With inflation driving up costs and interest rates climbing, avoiding late fees and high APRs is more important than ever.

By following this guide, you can ensure your Best Buy Credit Card payments are secure, timely, and hassle-free—no matter which method you choose.
